### 投资要点/ (Investment Highlights Subdirectory)

This subdirectory consolidates critical investment analysis materials prepared by Haitong International Securities specifically focused on the 2024 national Chinese patent medicine centralized procurement initiative. The collection encompasses multiple dimensions of analysis essential for informed investment decision-making in the Chinese medicine pharmaceutical sector.

**Policy Analysis and Interpretation**: Documents within this section provide detailed breakdowns of the 2024 centralized procurement policies, explaining regulatory frameworks, procurement procedures, and policy implications for enterprises and investors. These materials help stakeholders understand the structural changes and operational requirements introduced by the new procurement system.

**Product Evaluation System**: The directory includes comprehensive documentation of the evaluation methodologies and criteria used to assess Chinese patent medicine products. This covers quality standards, efficacy assessments, pricing mechanisms, and competitive positioning frameworks that determine product success in the centralized procurement environment.

**Enterprise Ranking Analysis**: Detailed rankings and comparative analyses of participating enterprises are provided, offering insights into market positioning, competitive advantages, product portfolios, and performance metrics. These rankings serve as critical reference points for investors evaluating potential investment targets and market opportunities.

**Compliance Disclosure Documents**: The subdirectory contains mandatory compliance statements and legal disclaimers tailored for investors in different geographic regions. These documents address regulatory requirements, risk disclosures, and investor protection measures, ensuring that all investment materials meet applicable legal and compliance standards across multiple jurisdictions.
