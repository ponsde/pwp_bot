### 公司信息更新报告2024年利润增长亮眼研发并购丰富产品矩阵/

This primary subdirectory contains the official 2024 company information update report for Lingrei Pharmaceutical, published by Kaiyuan Securities Research Institute. The report delivers a comprehensive analysis covering multiple dimensions of the company's operations and strategic direction.

**Financial Performance Highlights:** The report showcases exceptional financial results for 2024, with net income attributable to parent company reaching RMB 723 million, representing a year-over-year growth of 27.19%. Gross margin expanded to 75.82%, demonstrating improved operational efficiency and pricing power. Total revenue reached RMB 3.5 billion, reflecting solid top-line growth across the company's business segments.

**Product Portfolio Analysis:** The report provides detailed segmentation of product performance. Plaster products (贴剂) maintained stable and reliable performance, serving as a core revenue driver. Tablet products (片剂) demonstrated strong growth momentum, emerging as a key growth engine. Ointment products (软膏剂) experienced a slight decline, suggesting potential market saturation or competitive pressures in this segment.

**Dual-Engine Growth Strategy:** The company pursues growth through two complementary mechanisms. First, internal R&D innovation drives the development of new formulations and therapeutic solutions. Second, the strategic acquisition of Yingu Pharmaceutical (银谷制药) expands the product portfolio with high-barrier-to-entry products, including nasal spray formulations (鼻喷剂) and inhalation products (吸入剂), which command premium pricing and market positioning.

**Investment Recommendation:** The report maintains a "Buy" rating, supported by financial forecasts projecting continued growth potential through 2025-2027, indicating sustained momentum in the company's business trajectory.
