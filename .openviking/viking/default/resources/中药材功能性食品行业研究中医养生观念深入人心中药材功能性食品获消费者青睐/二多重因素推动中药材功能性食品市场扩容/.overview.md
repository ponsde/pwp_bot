### 1中国步入深度老龄化社会老龄人群基数庞大/ (China's Deep Aging Society and Large Elderly Population Base)

This subdirectory consolidates a comprehensive series of research reports on the development of the silver economy within China's aging society context. The collection covers multiple specialized segments including health supplements, dietary wellness and nourishment, and the traditional Chinese medicine industry. 

The materials provide in-depth analysis of elderly consumer characteristics, including their consumption preferences, purchasing power, health concerns, and lifestyle choices. Market scale assessments quantify the size and growth potential of various functional food segments targeting older demographics. Policy support analysis examines government initiatives, regulatory frameworks, and incentive mechanisms that facilitate industry development.

The subdirectory includes detailed industry chain mapping that traces the complete value chain from raw material sourcing (particularly Chinese medicinal materials) through processing, product development, distribution, and retail. This structural analysis helps stakeholders understand competitive positioning and identify value creation opportunities.

The content employs rigorous data analysis methodologies, including market penetration rate calculations, demographic segmentation studies, and trend forecasting. Consumption upgrade patterns are documented to show how elderly consumers are increasingly seeking premium, specialized, and scientifically-validated health products.
