## Detailed Description

### 1_引入国资大股东开展多元化业务/

This subdirectory aggregates in-depth research materials on a pharmaceutical listed company's introduction of state-owned capital shareholders and promotion of equity restructuring and diversified business development. The content encompasses equity structure adjustments, employee stock ownership plans, share repurchase mechanisms, and development dynamics of core business segments including TCM preparations, herbal medicine slices, and pharmaceutical R&D outsourcing (CXO) services. 

Particularly noteworthy is the significant improvement trend in gross margin for the herbal medicine slice business, which increased from 9.31% to 49.97%, demonstrating substantial operational efficiency gains. The directory also includes forward-looking analysis of the pharmaceutical research and development services business. The materials are designed for investors seeking to understand equity restructuring impacts, securities analysts conducting company valuation, enterprise management evaluating strategic direction, pharmaceutical industry researchers tracking sector trends, and professionals interested in pharmaceutical industry M&A and consolidation activities.

**Core keywords**: state-owned shareholders, equity changes, diversified business, TCM preparations, herbal medicine slices, CXO business, gross margin improvement, pharmaceutical research services.
